Project Loose Screw

A 3D obstacle game with a 1Vs1 LAN multiplayer style. It is composed of two levels, an option to select characters and at the end of each level the winner is announced


Team role

We were a team of 4. The four of us brainstormed the concept, I was responsible for level planning and the trap function, as well as the player movement.
